Video Player

Advanced video player supporting all formats (MP4, MKV, AVI, FLV, etc.) with custom controls, playback speed, subtitles, and fullscreen mode.

Music Player

Premium audio player with rotating album art, equalizer, playlists, and support for all audio formats including high-quality FLAC and MP3.

  • Rotating album art display

  • Playlist management
  • Audio formats support
  • Local library access
